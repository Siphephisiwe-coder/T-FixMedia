Common Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Call us directly and we'll answer any question.

Most repairs — including screen replacements, battery swaps, and charging port fixes — are completed same-day, often within 1–3 hours. More complex repairs like water damage or board-level work may take 24–48 hours. We'll always give you a realistic time estimate upfront before starting.
Yes. All our repairs come with a warranty covering both the parts and workmanship. If the same issue recurs within the warranty period, we'll fix it at no extra charge. Warranty duration varies by repair type — ask us for specifics when you drop your device off.
Repair costs vary depending on the device model and the type of repair needed. We provide a free diagnosis and give you a transparent quote before any work begins. There are no hidden fees — the price we quote is the price you pay, guaranteed.
If we're unable to repair your device, you pay nothing. Our "no fix, no fee" policy means the diagnosis is always free. We'll be honest with you about whether a repair is economically viable versus replacing the device — we'll never waste your money.
For most hardware repairs (screen, battery, charging port), your data is completely safe. However, for software repairs, factory resets, or severe water damage, data loss is sometimes unavoidable. We always recommend backing up your device beforehand, and we'll advise you before any risky procedure.
We use OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) or high-quality OEM-equivalent parts for all repairs. We never use low-grade substitutes that could affect your device's performance. If there's a choice between part grades, we'll explain the options and let you decide.
Absolutely. "Dead" devices can have many causes — a depleted battery, faulty charging circuit, software corruption, or board-level damage. Bring it in and we'll diagnose the root cause. Many devices that appear completely dead are fully repairable.
We repair all major brands including Samsung, Apple iPhone, Huawei, Xiaomi, Oppo, Vivo, Nokia, HP, Dell, Lenovo, Acer, Asus, and more. If you're unsure about your brand or model, just call us and we'll confirm before you make the trip.
Act fast — do NOT try to charge or turn it on, as this can short-circuit the board. Power it off immediately, remove any case, and bring it to us as soon as possible. Do NOT put it in rice — this is a myth and wastes valuable time. The sooner you bring it in, the higher the chance of a successful repair.
Walk-ins are welcome! However, booking ahead via our contact form or by calling us ensures we have the right parts ready and can prioritize your repair. For complex or less common repairs, we recommend calling first to confirm parts availability.
